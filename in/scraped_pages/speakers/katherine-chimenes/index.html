Name: Katherine Chimenes

Company: Price and Myers

Job Title: Structural Engineer

Biography

Katherine Chimenes is a structural engineer at Price & Myers with a strong focus on technology in the built environment. As a leader of the in-house Computation Group, Katherine drives innovation through automation and digital optimisation, enabling more efficient and sustainable design solutions. Katherineâ€™s diverse portfolio includes a wide range of projects ranging from public art programs and 24m tall collapsible parasols to the Evangelist spires of La Sagrada Familia and the Redevelopment of Euston Conventional Station with Grimshaw.

Being driven to promote computational skills in the AEC industry, Katherine has been a spokesperson across the industry advocating computational tools built for their users. Katherine initially studied Computing at Imperial College before switching to Engineering at UCL and teaches at the Architectural Association and the Bartlett School of Architecture.
